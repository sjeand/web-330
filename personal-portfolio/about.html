About Me

Hello! My name is Sarah Jean Baptiste. I am a web-development student at Bellevue University. When the COVID pandemic started I decided it was time to finally finish my bachelor degree. When I get my degree I would love to be a remote front-end engineer. I love learning new languages and programs but my true love is front-end. I wanted to choose a degree where I could still make a living but one where I can also express myself and show off my artistic abilities. I want to work remote, because my best job in life is being a wife and a mother. My husband and I have been married for six years and have a beautiful and spunky three year old. We love relaxing, hanging out at home, and just being together. While we are usually home-bodies, we love nothing more than traveling and showing our daughter the world.
